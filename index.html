AI FormFill Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2025

Overview

AI FormFill is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension operates entirely locally in your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers. Our primary goal is to help you fill out web forms quickly and accurately while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security.

Data Collection and Usage

Our extension collects and processes the following data:

  • Form Field Data: We analyze form fields on web pages to provide smart form filling suggestions. This analysis happens entirely in your browser.
  • User Preferences: We store your preferences for theme, form templates, and settings locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • User Activity: We track your interactions with the extension to improve form filling suggestions. This data is stored locally and never transmitted.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This includes:

  • Form templates and preferences
  • Theme settings
  • User interaction data

No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Protection

We implement the following security measures:

  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • User preferences are encrypted when stored
  • Regular security audits of the codebase

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data through Chrome's extension settings
  • Delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension
  • Opt out of data collection by disabling the extension
  • Export your stored data at any time

Third-Party Services

Our extension does not use any third-party services or analytics. All functionality is self-contained and operates entirely within your browser.
